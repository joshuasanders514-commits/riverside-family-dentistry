Meet the Team

Two dentists. One practice. One commitment.

Dr. Mitchell and Dr. Carter founded Riverside Family Dentistry on a simple belief: patients deserve a dental home where they feel genuinely cared for.

Doctor of Dental Surgery
Dr. James Mitchell
General & Cosmetic Dentistry

Dr. Mitchell has been practicing dentistry in Austin for over twelve years. He completed his dental training at the University of Texas Health Science Center and went on to complete advanced training in cosmetic and restorative dentistry. He is known among patients for his calm, unhurried approach and his ability to put even the most anxious patients at ease.

Doctor of Dental Surgery
Dr. Michael Carter
Family & Restorative Dentistry

Dr. Carter grew up in Central Texas and always planned to bring his dental career back to Austin. After completing his degree at Baylor College of Dentistry, he joined Dr. Mitchell to co-found Riverside Family Dentistry.
