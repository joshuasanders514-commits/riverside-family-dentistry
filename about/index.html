Meet the Team

Two doctors. One mission.
Your family's best smile.

Dr. James Mitchell and Dr. Michael Carter founded Riverside Family Dentistry on a belief that great dental care starts with a genuine relationship between doctor and patient.

Doctor of Dental Surgery
Dr. James Mitchell
General & Cosmetic Dentistry

Dr. Mitchell has been practicing dentistry in Austin for over twelve years. After completing his dental training at the University of Texas Health Science Center, he went on to pursue advanced coursework in cosmetic and restorative dentistry. He is known for his calm, patient-first approach and his particular ability to help anxious patients feel at ease from the very first appointment.

Dr. Mitchell grew up in San Antonio and came to Austin for dental school, falling in love with the city and its community. He coaches his son's youth soccer team on weekends and is deeply involved in Austin's community dental health initiatives.

Doctor of Dental Surgery
Dr. Michael Carter
Family & Restorative Dentistry

Dr. Carter grew up in Waco and always planned to bring his dental career back to Central Texas. After completing his dental degree at Baylor College of Dentistry, he joined Dr. Mitchell to co-found Riverside Family Dentistry. Dr. Carter has a special passion for working with families and children, including patients who have never been to the dentist before.

Outside the office, Dr. Carter is an avid fly fisherman and hiker. He and his wife have two young children who are, naturally, patients at Riverside. He believes that a practice that treats the whole family builds something that lasts for generations.

Our Philosophy

The values we built this practice on.

Every decision we make, from how we design appointments to how we talk to patients, comes back to these three things.

People Before Procedures

We treat patients, not just teeth. Understanding who you are and what you need always comes first in our practice.

Honesty in Every Recommendation

We only recommend treatment you genuinely need. We will always tell you what can wait and what cannot, without any pressure.

Care That Lasts a Lifetime

We are building a practice where families come for decades. That long-term relationship shapes every decision we make.
