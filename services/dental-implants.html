Permanent Tooth Replacement

Dental implants in Austin that look, feel, and function like natural teeth.

Dental implants are the gold standard for replacing missing teeth. They are the only tooth replacement option that actually replaces the root of the tooth, preserving your jawbone and giving you a result that can last a lifetime.

Overview

Dental implants in Austin that look, feel, and function like natural teeth.

If you are missing one or more teeth, dental implants offer a solution that no other option can match. Unlike dentures, implants do not slip or require adhesives. Unlike bridges, they do not require grinding down healthy neighboring teeth. An implant is a small titanium post placed in your jawbone that fuses with the bone over time, creating a foundation as strong as a natural tooth root. The crown placed on top is custom-made to match your surrounding teeth in color, shape, and size.

Single tooth implants for individual missing teeth
Implant-supported bridges for multiple adjacent missing teeth
Implant-supported dentures for full arch replacement
Implant crown restoration (we work with your oral surgeon)
Bone grafting evaluation and referral when needed
Full consultation and treatment planning

Am I a candidate for dental implants?

Most healthy adults are candidates for dental implants. The main requirements are adequate bone density in your jaw and healthy gums. Patients with certain health conditions including uncontrolled diabetes or who smoke heavily may need additional evaluation. The only way to know for certain is a comprehensive exam and imaging, which we will do at your consultation.

Common Questions

Frequently asked questions about Dental Implants.

How long does the implant process take?

The full implant process typically takes four to six months from placement to final crown. This is because the implant post needs time to fuse with your jawbone in a process called osseointegration. While this sounds lengthy, most of that time is healing, not treatment. Patients are typically back to normal life within a few days of each procedure.

Do dental implants require special care?

No special tools or products are needed. You brush and floss implants exactly as you would natural teeth. Regular exams and cleanings are important to monitor the implant and keep the surrounding gum tissue healthy. With proper care, implants can last 20 to 30 years or more.
